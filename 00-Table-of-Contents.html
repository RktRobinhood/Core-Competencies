    Developing Learners, Developing Individuals

    ◉ About This Resource

    This textbook brings together research on core competencies, cognitive science, pedagogy, and educational philosophy to support the expansion of IB Theory of Knowledge from 100 to 150 hours. The additional time focuses on developing students as learners and as individuals — grounding that development in evidence, not assumption.
